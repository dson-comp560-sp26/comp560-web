# Attendance policy 

<font color="red">The following policy was announced on March 23, 2026. It applies only to the second half of the semester.</font>

Attendance at the weekly meeting is required. Excused absences, when requested in advance, will be granted on a lenient basis. Unexcused absences or overall meeting attendance below 75% will likely result in a reduced grade. Students with a scheduling conflict for the weekly meeting should contact the instructors to make alternative arrangements.
